Members of the band

Its members are M. Shadows (vocals), Synyster Gates (lead guitar and backing vocals), Zacky Vengeance (rhythm guitar and backing vocals), Johnny Christ (bass). Previously, the drummer and vocalist was The Rev until his death in December 2009.

M.Shadow

Matthew Charles Sanders better known as M. Shadows, is the lead vocalist of the heavy metal band Avenged Sevenfold. In his teens, M.Shadows played basketball, but ended up giving it up when his interest in music was sparked. Along with The Rev and Zacky Vengeance.Also on the "All Excess" DVD, Shadows states that all the band members had made a list of names for the band but according to him they chose the weirdest. The Avenged Sevenfold name, a reference to the book Genesis, was chosen for "Lips Of Deceit", another Biblical reference and which became a song on the album Sounding the Seventh Trumpet.

Synester Gates

Brian Elwin Haner Jr. better known by his stage name Synyster Gates, is an American guitarist known for being the lead guitarist and backing vocalist for the Heavy band. Metal, Avenged Sevenfold. Brian was born in Huntington Beach, Orange County, California. His first guitar was given to him by his grandparents. He met the rest of the band through Jimmy The Rev Sullivan, they realized they were both musicians and became almost inseparable friends. He admits openly that he was not good at school. Brian graduated from and attended the Musicians Institute in Hollywood as part of the Guitar Institute Of Technology program, the study of jazz guitar. Until then he was mainly self-taught by watching music videos and reading books, which is surprising because his father is such a great songwriter and guitarist.

Zacky Vengeance

Zachary James Baker better known by his stage name Zacky Vengeance, is the rhythm guitarist, second voice, and co-founder of the American heavy metal band Avenged Sevenfold. He began his career in a punk band that he formed,which he called MPA* where he was the lead vocalist and guitarist. On April 20, 2011, he won the Revolver Golden God Award for Best Guitarist with his bandmate, lead guitarist Synyster Gates.

Jhonny Christ

Jonathan Lewis Seward better known by his stage name Johnny Christ, professional bassist and occasional American chorus girl, began playing while he was in high school in a group formed by him and his best friend back then. He is most recognized for having replaced the late Dameon Ash, being the fourth and current bassist as well as backing vocalist with guitarist Zacky Vengeance of the Californian heavy metal band Avenged Sevenfold.

The Rev

James Owen "Jimmy" Sullivan more popularly known by his stage name The Reverend Tholomew Plague (shortened to The Rev), He was an American musician, writer, songwriter, and instrumentalist specializing in hard rock and heavy metal, most recognized for being the second vocalist, drummer, songwriter, pianist, and founder of the heavy metal band Avenged Sevenfold. He began his career using the ska genre as the co-founder and drummer of the band Suburban Legends during 1998-1999. He also formed an avant-garde metal and punk rock band called Pinkly Smooth in 2001 where he was known under the name "Rathead" where he was the pianist and vocalist.
